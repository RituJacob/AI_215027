BANKING BOT

Banking Bot has been designed to cater to customer inquiries regarding the bank and its comprehensive range of products and services. These services encompass activities from opening bank accounts to applying for loans and exploring various digital banking offerings. The chatbot's intelligence is the result of training on a specialized dataset, and it's powered by a deep learning model integrated into the PyTorch framework. To ensure a robust understanding of customer queries, Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques such as stemming, tokenization, and lemmatization were applied to the dataset, which consists of sentences stored in a .json file. As a result of this training and NLP capabilities, BankBot is now proficient in providing accurate and helpful responses to various questions posed by the bank's customers inquiring about their services.
